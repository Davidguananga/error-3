About Us

For nearly three decades, Flamenco Flavors has been known as one of New York’s leading Spanish restaurants, applauded for both its gracious service and alluring take on fine dining. Flamenco Flavors is an ode to Executive Chef Jesus Martinez’s love for presenting the flavors of his country + providing his guests with an exceptional dining experience that speaks to his humble beginnings on the Spanish countryside. Our menu changes seasonally to reflect the bounty of fresh, local ingredients from land and sea.
